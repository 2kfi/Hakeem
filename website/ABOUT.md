# About Hakeem AI

Hakeem AI is a project born from a passion for open-source technology and a desire to create a beautiful, intuitive, and private interface for interacting with Large Language Models (LLMs). Our goal is to provide a self-hostable chat application that puts you in control of your data and your AI experience.

---

## Our Philosophy

We believe that the power of modern AI should be accessible to everyone, without compromising on privacy or user experience. 

*   **Privacy-First:** Your conversations are yours. Hakeem is designed to connect to local or private LLMs, ensuring your data never leaves your control. We store chat history directly in your browser's `localStorage`.
*   **Beautiful & Intuitive Design:** A great user experience is central to our design. We've crafted a clean, modern, and responsive interface that feels natural to use, with a stunning dark mode and subtle animations.
*   **Flexibility:** Hakeem is not tied to a single model provider. With a configurable backend, you can connect to a local `llama.cpp` server, commercial APIs like Google Gemini, or any other compatible endpoint.

## The Technology

This application is built with a simple and effective stack:

*   **Backend:** A lightweight **Flask** (Python) server handles routing and serves the frontend application.
*   **Frontend:** The UI is built with vanilla **HTML, CSS, and JavaScript**, keeping it fast, simple, and easy to modify. No complex frameworks are needed.
*   **Styling:** We use modern CSS with variables for easy theming and a clean, maintainable structure.
